History

Chicago has a rich and lengthy history, evident in its famous architecture and other key parts of the cityscape.

Food

Food in Chicago is renowned worldwide for its diversity of origin and flavor. You can eat food from any culture in the world in and around the city.

Visiting Chicago

Nightlife

Chicago's nightlife varies greatly depending on the neighborhood, but there are events every weekend and weeknight that keep the city busy after dark.

Culture

Chicago's culture has been defined by the presence of arts and the development of multiple ethnic and cultural groups alonside one another.
